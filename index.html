Welcome to WhatsMyPC

This little page gives you an overview about the most common parts of a PC. Every component has its own little article, explaining you what the component is, what it does and where it is located in a PC. There are also additional infos with further reading and interesting little details. This page is not meant to be a big PC guide. The information is kept short and gives you a brief introduction to the single components.
You can start your journey with any component you like the order is not important. They all work together and have their own relationship with each other. You can also expand the navigation menu at the top right of the page to navigate to any part or to the homescreen back here.
