Motherboard

What is it and what does it do?

This video from Power Cert Animated Videos gives a good overview of a motherboard and its functions

Where is it?

The motherboard is the big circuit board in the middle of the PC case and can be easily identified. It can come in many different colors and in different sizes and is directly visible through a possible window in the PC or when opening up. The image to the right shows the position of the motherboard.

Additional info

The motherboard has its own software called BIOS (Basic Input/Output System) which can be accessed before the startup of the operating system. The amount of settings in the motherboard depend on the manufacturer

Below is a small table with the most common keys for different manufacturers to access the BIOS.

Manufacturer ASRock ASUS Acer Dell Gigabyte/Aorus Lenovo HP MSI Samsung Toshiba Zotac
Possible Keys F2 or DEL F2 or DEL F2 or DEL F2 or F12 F2 or DEL F2, Fn + F12, F1 or Enter + F1 F10 DEL F2 F2 DEL
