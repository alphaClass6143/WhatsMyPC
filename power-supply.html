Power Supply

What is it and what does it do?

In simple terms: The power supply is responsible for the power delivery for the PC. The power supply converts AC to DC.

Where is it?

The position of the power supply can vary by case. It is usually located at the bottom back of the PC. The power cable connector and the On/Off switch on the outside of the case, gives its position away. The picture to the right shows its usual position in the case. The cable are highlighted in red.
